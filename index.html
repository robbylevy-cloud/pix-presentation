The Unlock
Leverage on Prediction Markets.
The Architectural Breakthrough.

Everyone in the prediction market space knows leverage is the killer unlock. It is what turns a niche product into an institutional asset class. But every attempt to bolt leverage onto raw binary contracts hits the same wall.

The Problem

Prediction market contracts move 10%, 20%, 50%, even 80% in a heartbeat. A price move from 60¢ to 30¢ wipes out any position at 2× leverage. The insurance fund math does not work. Socialized loss destroys the experience for participants on the right side of the trade.

This has been a structural dead end for prediction markets. The industry knows leverage is a monumental unlock, but nobody has found the architecture to support it.

The Solution: PIX

PIX is an index. A continuous, smooth, mean-reverting number derived from a portfolio of underlying contracts across a strip of barriers and tenors. The individual 100s contracts underneath can jump wildly. But PIX, because it synthesizes across the strip, smooths those jumps into a continuous surface that moves in an orderly, modelable fashion.

That means you can run a perpetual futures contract on PIX with standard margin, standard liquidation engines, and a clearinghouse guarantee fund that is actually sizeable against real worst-case scenarios. The worst case is structurally bounded and the path to get there is smooth enough that the liquidation engine can actually function.

The underlying 100s contracts remain fully collateralized. No leverage. No margin calls. No liquidation. That is Tier 1, the bedrock. PIX-PERP sits on top as Tier 2, offering leverage on a bounded, continuous, mean-reverting index that feeds off the liquid markets of fully collateralized components beneath it. The leverage does not touch the foundation. The foundation makes the leverage safe.
